"Ertereya Kunama Koibiša Dimokrasiya Suňada (Erkukodisu) Agastana Komita Summada Akontabara Kosayda Faneda"

Published 2016-03-22

Ertereyana Agariyana Higdef Usulana:Ertereya Kunama koibiša Dimokrasiya Suňada (ERKUKODISU) agastana komitasummadiya issega akontabara kiggaski ojinabbu tillaske.

Inna summadenalle Ertereya košere abaya kišama šabiya akumata košere baddala kele gosuma mengelowa konasi kitaf gosumowana botata furdabbu kosabbi kišaki dimokrasiya aca kinamma marabbu kosodaki Ertereya ajinnasi lajinata kifegedamowa jarimata taggima kišamowa kiki kišama kulabbu gadibiya kifegedanasi oššš mana lakaski harrr mana gasuna hirasi koke. Aba kalla nakosso dabbuna danta kotikabbu košera ňaňale baddala kiggas kišima yafadana mabusa lilana mena kinamma suluba (suma) bu kaya kišima fanakowa koddi gasumowana ordiniyabbu oina jarimatowayela kokotaf košime masulatine bubiyesi sababa aňňadiyebbu kiya faski mabusa kutuki asaritta kibal fas goske. Sellasi lagam leesuma amelana alaloda itata kokkidama wokitala oina sanowa tillamme ayniyebbu kokoya fanke, aw akumata ordinakin adagata kišama tabilabbu kokosodamowabbu baddiyala fada koma makke sunasina imebbu affenas kišaki gadibiya kussu kikana ondobebbu kosoda gosuma Erkukodisu agastana komita yii kišake. Ay ondobebbu košigidayana jarimatinakin kobesannima kišamowana mari marowa italalike. Inna baddala usula bayena Kunama košerasi Ertereya arsibakin šillay seles kolanasi allebbu turka kiliski ottabbu turka kiweki botatiyabbu lesuma toma bibotta kiki kutunasi kossa bubiya kimalamowana deda saganne iwe ininiňe afunakin battan koinammesi turka kiweki talima waga kikino ašiye lawabbu hena kokobin kosamowana mari marowa yi kišake. Innana Kunama kitadana kiširgama ondobowayekin ella kišamowana akumatowa jarimatiya baddala jarimata kitaf gosumowana agastana komitowa italalike. Awra etetabbu šabiya akumata koyiya alemateda babara uyella kika kinamma, kosoda kinamma tabilena kanunesi titoski gajodala kuwamowa mari marowa yi kišake.

Ertereya bagana kittite farda kililikimabbu:- Erkukodisu agastana komita Ertereya bagana kittite fardala kofegeda tame okonti gommowana laweta tokorana abayeyana nissala mallayta katawama tabilabbu tayta aw sadiyata tame osa kolo gommowana inna taytenaye tiriba šabiya akumata fungadasi animinda mayde kosamowana kintike medanoske. Innakin diyadatabbu siyasatiya nissa aňňadida kos gosu ide cartera aňňade inna gomu lakin šabiya akumata fungadasi bagana kittite bubiye sokomasuma hella tufemunasi niyatana kisbatana kosimowa ayiba maidowana ekkenowana kišamowa kimmi kidorke. Kittite kontalle sadiyatana doriyasi gooda ekkenala kokosima ajende tillaski kurtogida kosama taršata kinalenasi kossu gosumowana fattašoske. Ay fanakala kittite kontalle sadiyatowa hegeweski ika gosuma fada komena inola gosuma fanede giitinena alalena kanunala kokotinkišama serga tokona badda šuttata bulaski išabki inolame ditta kotikabbu kokoširgame kosammowana marimarowa kabbateski inna ayibenabbu kosodaki Ertereyala kofegeda kikonowa tiridakosimmowa kimmi kidorke. Inna kittite kontalle fardena košera kittite nafasina konandasina kišamowana kimmi kidorke.

Erkukodisu mari-mara galla kelida 2015 Kunama kittita uddodossi tagamena faguda taggime oke košima kišayana katirabbuna nadifatos kišama kittitoda ayibana agasamaidaba kišama marimarana košita lagana igidiňňala kokosima nissala mallaytena agarette kalma kinandi kišimowabbu turba bubiye taraski batafalabbu fos kišamowana kitalalike. Erkukodisu galla kelida 1995 kin ašik 2015 agasala kosima baca okitenalle nissalabbuna fitetabbu Afrika gilala mallayta kanka kinandi kišimowabbu min košitiyakin Kunamala koinama kokkana askamana agiyala kokki yin koloma šabiya akumatana širkatiyena Kunama na kittitiya uddoda Erkukodisusi kotubbu titosuna niyatowana matarebowa tillada kitemmowana sanabbu kontike. Inna tagamenaye sarodala kišano turbowaye taradala ekkenabbu Erkukodisu olkomala kittitoski kittitiyasi adugaba lakasuma agarana, nissalabbu kakotaytama Ertereya kittita angudena, mariyena, Ihwedegna Ihwedeg bu konadi gosuma Ethiopia agarana akumatiyana homma arbowassi Erkukodisu mari marowa kidabbu aftatiya lewes goske. Tafabbuna, Ertereyana Kunama košera lajinossi hella sisima kišomossina kokašabbu aftata kišoke. Inna tayta keddusena bobanda nissala kekowa Ertereya arsibalana buusubbu siyasata elate bariye Afrika gilala arba homuna kišamowana tiridittabbu kimmi kidorke.

Ertereya Kunamana Kittitiya uddoda Erkukodisuna Mikosima Damana:Erkukodisu mari mara komaldiya hegewedala Ertereya Kunama kasakala konti gosuma furdena saguda jole okosimowabbu kogošama kofegedowa fanaka etetala koitemmowana bacana sana mema koludamowana medanoske. Išaditta Kunama košera babara uyella koskamaki ka šuttala kommi kinama fanakolle kina kinama abayowaye kalmiyabb kišano kelediyabbu anda angude kokos koinamowana Kunamossina kolliya aw baddiyala kišammowabbu goola šukkarende komeki osaro koinamowana kintiki tammake Erkukodisu marabbu Kunamowa targadana kotoronana siyasata kalmana kinandimowabbu seriya komusunasi hemma elatesi kišoma kasidowabbu kosa koloma abaye kelediyana kokkiya hira bayala kimowana kikišake. Abaya anguda ninaki ka šuttala harodakinna koskamakin abaya alabena fodena okosibbu oššaliyabbu dannodiyana ankatiyana giitiyasi bacowa ayiba ekkenowa kišamowana ille kidorke. Inna lilikena sulubaňa ankataňa irawandabbu kabbatesumowabbu Kunamiňňa košerana ankata kiteke. Inna ayibena kišano ankatena koitemowa agasa maidaba kišama mari mara (core leadership) kinandimowabbu kišamowa ammata maidabbu hegewes kidorke. Inna marena išiima talenena anis košima mara gangamowa asarabbu kokosodanasi mari mara siyasatana tillada kalma gootowa oššalina kišama tabila kišamowabbu laga sasala kokotakema kitabekinna Kunama košera kososonette sausuma marimara conferensa kussuki marimara siyasata kalmowa budedasi kisananowana kimmi kidorke. Mari marala kontima kofegedokowa deya Kunama agarolle kofegeda kikona suma lilikowana kosasammabbu kisasaki inna hirenassi kikoma agarossi haroda kalla ayiba andana maidana kosimmowana kekiyabbu kimmi kidorke. Siyasata kalmabbu košigidamma mari mara kokofegeda kikonnimowana ketawa kitakki maktabe kussumiye sana reportena kintiki tiribiyana dekediyena medanoske. Tele kontalle sana matarebana kiširgaki summadiyana fardowa ankatinabbu kiseke.

Kabo kakoma Koibiša bacowa komaldiya hirata kakina!

Tamana Erkukodisu Nissale Aritomuna!

Marso, 2016
